Company Overview

Learn about our company's history, values, and commitment to excellence.

Our Story

Founded in 1998, Saron Inc. has grown from a small regional storage provider to a global leader in the tank storage industry. Our journey began with a single facility in Texas, and today we operate over 15 strategically located facilities across three continents.

Our growth has been driven by a commitment to excellence, safety, and customer service. We continually invest in our infrastructure, technology, and people to ensure that we provide the highest quality storage solutions to our clients.

As we look to the future, we remain dedicated to expanding our capacity, enhancing our capabilities, and setting new industry standards for safety, efficiency, and environmental responsibility.

Our Values

The principles that guide our operations and decisions every day.

Safety First

We prioritize the safety of our employees, contractors, and communities in every decision we make. Our commitment to safety is non-negotiable and embedded in our culture.

Environmental Stewardship

We are committed to minimizing our environmental footprint through responsible operations, sustainable practices, and continuous improvement in our environmental performance.

Customer Focus

We strive to understand and meet our customers' needs by providing reliable, flexible, and efficient storage solutions that contribute to their success.

Operational Excellence

We pursue excellence in all aspects of our operations through rigorous standards, continuous improvement, and the adoption of best practices and technologies.

Team Collaboration

We believe in the power of teamwork, diversity, and inclusion. We foster a collaborative environment where everyone's contributions are valued and respected.

Integrity and Transparency

We conduct our business with the highest ethical standards, honesty, and transparency in all our interactions with stakeholders, customers, and employees.

Company Milestones

Key achievements in our journey to becoming an industry leader.

1998

Saron Inc. founded with our first storage facility in Texas with a capacity of 100,000 barrels.

2003

Expanded operations to Louisiana with the acquisition of Gulf Storage Solutions.

2008

Celebrated 10 years in business with 5 facilities and over 1 million barrels of storage capacity.

2012

International expansion with the opening of our first facility in Rotterdam, Netherlands.

2016

Achieved ISO 14001 certification for all North American facilities.

2018

Celebrated 20th anniversary and surpassed 5 million barrels of total storage capacity.

2021

Expansion into Asia with new facilities in Singapore and South Korea.

2023

Opened our 15th facility and reached 10 million barrels of global storage capacity.
