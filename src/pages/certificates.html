Our Commitment to Excellence

At Saron Inc., we adhere to the highest industry standards and best practices across all our operations. Our certifications and accreditations reflect our unwavering commitment to quality, safety, environmental responsibility, and operational excellence.

We regularly undergo rigorous third-party audits to maintain these credentials, ensuring that our facilities and operations consistently meet or exceed regulatory requirements and industry benchmarks. These certifications are a testament to our dedication to providing our clients with storage solutions they can trust.

Industry-Specific Certifications

Specialized accreditations for the tank storage and energy sectors.

CDI-T (Chemical Distribution Institute - Terminals)

International standard for the inspection and audit of chemical storage terminals. Our facilities consistently achieve high ratings in CDI-T assessments, verifying our compliance with industry best practices for chemical storage.

ISPS Code Compliance

International Ship and Port Facility Security Code compliance for our marine terminals, ensuring comprehensive security measures to detect security threats and prevent security incidents affecting ships or port facilities.

API 653 Certification

American Petroleum Institute certification for tank inspection, repair, alteration, and reconstruction. Our technical teams include certified API 653 inspectors who ensure our storage tanks maintain structural integrity and operational safety.

NFPA Compliance

Compliance with National Fire Protection Association standards for fire prevention and protection in tank storage facilities. Our facilities adhere to stringent fire safety protocols to minimize fire risks.

OSHA VPP Star Status

Voluntary Protection Programs recognition from the Occupational Safety and Health Administration for our U.S. facilities, recognizing exemplary worksite safety and health programs that go beyond OSHA standards.

ILTA Safety Awards

Multiple Safety Excellence Awards from the International Liquid Terminals Association, recognizing our outstanding safety performance and innovative safety programs across our terminal network.

Our Certification Process

How we maintain the highest standards across all our operations.

1

Integrated Management System

We maintain a comprehensive integrated management system (IMS) that aligns our quality, environmental, safety, and energy management processes. This integrated approach ensures consistency across all operations and simplifies compliance with multiple standards.

2

Regular Internal Audits

Our qualified internal audit team conducts scheduled and surprise audits across all facilities to verify compliance with standards and identify opportunities for improvement. These internal assessments help maintain continuous compliance between third-party certification audits.

3

Third-Party Verification

All our facilities undergo regular third-party audits by accredited certification bodies to verify compliance with international standards and maintain certifications. These independent assessments provide objective verification of our management systems.

4

Continuous Improvement

We implement a rigorous continuous improvement process that incorporates findings from internal and external audits, industry developments, and stakeholder feedback. This approach ensures our systems evolve to address emerging challenges and opportunities.

5

Training and Awareness

Regular training ensures all employees understand their roles in maintaining compliance with certified management systems. We foster a culture where quality, safety, and environmental responsibility are everyone's responsibility.
