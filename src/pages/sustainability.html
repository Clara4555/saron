Our Commitment to Sustainability

At Saron Inc., sustainability is not just a corporate objective but a core value that guides our business operations, strategic decisions, and future planning. We recognize our responsibility to minimize environmental impact while delivering reliable storage solutions to our clients.

As a leader in the tank storage industry, we are committed to developing and implementing innovative practices that promote environmental stewardship, social responsibility, and economic prosperity. Our comprehensive sustainability approach addresses every aspect of our operations, from facility design and construction to daily operational practices.

Through continuous improvement and investment in technology, we strive to set new industry standards for sustainable tank storage solutions. We believe that responsible business practices not only benefit the environment and communities where we operate but also create long-term value for our stakeholders.

Environmental Stewardship

Implementing advanced technologies and processes to minimize our ecological footprint and protect natural resources.

Social Responsibility

Engaging with and supporting the communities where we operate, ensuring safety, and fostering diversity and inclusion.

Economic Prosperity

Creating sustainable growth and value for our stakeholders while maintaining the highest standards of corporate governance.

"Our commitment to sustainability is not just about compliance with regulations; it's about leading the industry in developing practices that protect our planet for future generations while providing the essential storage infrastructure needed today."

CEO, Saron Inc.

Environmental Initiatives

Our comprehensive approach to minimizing environmental impact across all operations.

Renewable Energy Integration

We are progressively transitioning our facilities to renewable energy sources, with solar panels installed at 65% of our locations and a goal to reach 100% by 2030. Our Rotterdam terminal already operates on 100% renewable electricity.

Emissions Reduction

Our advanced vapor recovery systems capture and process volatile organic compounds (VOCs), reducing air emissions by over 95%. We've implemented floating roof technologies across our facilities to minimize evaporation losses.

Water Conservation

Through water recycling systems and rainwater harvesting, we've reduced freshwater consumption by 40% since 2015. Our state-of-the-art water treatment facilities ensure that any discharged water meets or exceeds regulatory standards.

Waste Management

Our comprehensive waste management program focuses on reducing, reusing, and recycling materials across our operations. We've achieved a 75% waste diversion rate and continue to work toward our zero-waste-to-landfill goal.

Biodiversity Protection

We conduct thorough environmental impact assessments for all new projects and have implemented habitat restoration programs at existing facilities. Our Singapore terminal includes a 5-acre wildlife sanctuary managed in partnership with local conservation groups.

Sustainable Construction

All new facilities are designed according to LEED principles, incorporating energy-efficient systems, sustainable materials, and environmentally responsible construction practices. Our Houston expansion achieved LEED Gold certification in 2022.

Our Sustainability Goals

Measurable targets that guide our sustainability journey through 2030 and beyond.

2025

Near-Term Objectives

  • Reduce greenhouse gas emissions by 30% (compared to 2020 baseline)
  • Achieve zero reportable spills across all operations
  • Implement advanced leak detection and repair (LDAR) programs at all facilities
  • Reduce freshwater consumption by 50% (compared to 2015 baseline)
  • Achieve 80% waste diversion from landfills
2030

Mid-Term Objectives

  • Reduce greenhouse gas emissions by 60% (compared to 2020 baseline)
  • Transition to 100% renewable electricity across all operations
  • Implement circular economy principles for 90% of operational waste
  • Achieve water-neutral status in water-stressed regions
  • Convert 50% of company vehicle fleet to electric or hydrogen power
2050

Long-Term Vision

  • Achieve net-zero carbon emissions across all operations
  • Develop and implement closed-loop systems for all material flows
  • Establish regenerative environmental programs that create net-positive impact
  • Pioneer next-generation storage solutions for renewable and alternative energy products
  • Create biodiversity net gain on all our operational sites
